How to Claim

Redeem your rewards in 3 simple steps

1

For Videos

Send the video link via DM to a staff member. Allow 24 to 48 hours for verification.

2

For Streams

Record a short phone video showing your viewer count, gameplay, Discord link and hashtag for at least 5 seconds. No screenshots. Send it to a staff member via DM.

3

Get Your Rewards

Once verified, your Discord role and in-game rewards will be granted.
